Architecture

Connected in 48 hours. Not 48 weeks.

SYNTRA uses a pre-built connector library for the most common logistics systems and a universal API layer for everything else. Our team handles setup — you just tell us what you're running.

01

Connector discovery

We audit your current stack and map every data source — TMS, WMS, ERP, IoT, and partner APIs.

02

Pre-built connectors activated

Native connectors are activated with credentials — no custom development for 200+ supported systems.

03

Custom fields & event mapping

We map your data schema to SYNTRA's unified model — field by field, event by event.

04

Go live & validate

Test in staging, flip the switch, and start executing from one place within 48 hours.
