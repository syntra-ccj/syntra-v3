The 2500mAh Li-Po battery keeps the Beacon R running for up to 30 days on a single charge. Three intelligent power modes let you balance reporting frequency against battery life for any deployment scenario.

Offline Intelligence

Data That Never Gets Lost

Signal dead zones don't mean data gaps. The Beacon R stores up to 20,000 data entries locally — location, temperature, humidity, and alarm events — then syncs automatically when connectivity returns.

  • 20,000 exportable data entries
  • 5,000 GPS data entries (buffer)
  • Automatic sync on reconnection
  • Perfect for cold chain compliance auditing
