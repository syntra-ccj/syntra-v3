What we look for

People who think like operators.

We build for people who move real freight. The best people on our team understand that deeply.

🔍

Customer-first thinking

Every decision starts with: what does the person running freight at 2am actually need? If you don't ask that question naturally, we're probably not a fit.

⚡

Bias toward action

We move fast and we ship. We'd rather learn from a real outcome than spend three meetings aligning on a plan that never gets tested.

🧩

Systems-level thinking

Logistics is all interconnected. The best solutions here don't just solve the immediate problem — they make the surrounding system better too.

🤝

Direct communication

We say what we mean and mean what we say. No politics, no ambiguity, no passive-aggressive Slack messages. Just honest, clear communication.

📐

High craft, low ego

We care deeply about the quality of our work — and not at all about who gets credit for it. The best idea wins, regardless of where it came from.

🌱

Ownership mentality

Everyone here runs something. If something's broken, we fix it. If something's missing, we build it. We don't wait to be told what to do.
